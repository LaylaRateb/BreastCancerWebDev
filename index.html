What is LadyLife Wellness?

Breast cancer stands as a significant health concern for women globally. LadyLife Wellness is dedicated to providing education and support specifically tailored to those affected by breast cancer. Our platform offers resources and support to help women navigate their journey through diagnosis, treatment, and survivorship, reminding women that they are not alone.

Data

Breast cancer stands as the predominant form of cancer affecting women globally. It holds the title of the most frequently occurring cancer among women in 157 out of 185 countries across the world.

What do people who are diagnosed with breast cancer have in common?

People affected by breast cancer typically exhibit a common feature characterized by genetic variations, commonly referred to as mutations, present in the DNA of their breast cells. DNA, serving as the foundational genetic material of cells, functions as a blueprint containing directives that dictate the regulation of cell growth, governing both the initiation and cessation of cellular proliferation.

How common is breast cancer in the United states?

In the United States, the population of women with a breast cancer history surpasses four million, encompassing those undergoing treatment and those who have completed it.
